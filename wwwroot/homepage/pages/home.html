WHAT IS INHANDPLUS?

InHandPlus was established to contribute to the well-being of people by providing the convenient and efficient healthcare solution. Firstly, to solve the medication non-adherence problem, we develop medication-use smartwatch and AI-based medication management solution. Moreover, utilizing the real-world data collected by our solution, we plan to provide personalized healthcare service and medication insight.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create simple, effective and trustful healthcare solutions with high technology for a better life and well-being of people

OUR SOLUTION

Our proprietary smartwatch is able to tell what medicine users take, and when they take it. Our AI-based management platform collects the real-world data and provides personalized healthcare services and insights.
