                                WHAT IS INHANDPLUS?

                                InHandPlus, Inc. was established to contribute the well-being of people by providing the convenient and efficient healthcare solution. By focusing on the medication non-adherence problem, we develop medication-use smartwatch and AI based medication management platform. Moreover, utilizing the medication data collected by the smartwatch and platform, we plan to provide personalized healthcare service and medication insight.

                                WHY INHANDPLUS?

                                We generate a trustful real-world healthcare data using patient-centric digital platform and improve patient engagement for a better healthcare management reducing the medical cost for both payors and patients.
